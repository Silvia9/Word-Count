      Spring Festival or the Lunar New Year, is the most important festival of the Chinese traditional holidays. Excitement and happiness are palpable this time of the year, and they reach the peak on lunar new years eve.
 It starts with the first day of the lunar new year and ends on the day. Chinese people has been celebrating the Spring Festival for more than years. With various activities and the longest history. Its the most important, ceremonious and jolliest holiday in our country.
